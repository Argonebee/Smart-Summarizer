FAQ & About

What is Smart Summarizer?

A free tool for students to convert educational material (lectures, PDFs, videos) into concise notes and interactive flashcards using Gemini AI.

Which models are supported?

Currently uses Gemini gemini-1.5-flash for fast, accurate summarization and flashcard creation.

Is my data private?

Yes! All summarization happens in your browser. Your API key and content are not sent to any server other than Google's Gemini API.

How do I get a Gemini API key?

Sign up at Google AI Studio to obtain your key.

Can I export my notes?

Yes, you can export your summary and flashcards as .txt or .pdf files, and save them in your browser for later.
